🎨 Art Style Guide

Resolution

Base sprites are 32x32 pixels. Environment backgrounds are 256x144. All assets are pixel-perfect with no anti-aliasing.

Outline

Consistent 1px black outline on all sprites. Creates separation and readability against any background.

Shading

Minimum 3-tone shading: base color, highlight, and shadow. Limited palette per asset for consistency.

Proportions

Chibi-style characters are 2 heads tall. Items follow standardized sizing for inventory grid compatibility.

Rarity Colors

Common (Gray), Rare (Blue), Epic (Purple), Legendary (Gold/Rainbow)

Animation

4-frame cycles at 8fps. Idle breathing, working actions, and celebration animations for characters.
